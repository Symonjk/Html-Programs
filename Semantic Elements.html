Cats

Cats are small, domesticated mammals (Felis catus) known for their agility, independence, and playful yet predatory nature. They are popular pets that often form close bonds with humans while retaining many natural hunting instincts.

Facts about Cats

  1. Cats sleep around 12–16 hours a day.
  2. They can make over 100 different sounds.
  3. A cat’s purr can have a calming effect on humans.
  4. Cats always land on their feet thanks to their “righting reflex.”
  5. The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave in Cyprus.
  6. Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees.
  7. A group of cats is called a clowder.
  8. Their noses have unique patterns—like human fingerprints.
  9. They can run up to 30 km/h (about 18 mph).
